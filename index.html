Concept Projects

Stopgap

People hate when their flights are canceled. With insights gathered from interviews with four frequent travelers, I designed a travel app that recommends alternative flight, car and train routes when travel is interrupted.

Icebreaker

Networking is hard. Through five interviews conducted at a local coworking space, my teammate and I created an app to make introductions easier. The app uses bluetooth to connect you with people around you who have similiar interests.

Zilker Botanical Gardens

The 2010 ZBG website was busy, inconsistent and challenging to navigate. Through 10 in-person interviews with users and stakeholders we aligned on a redesign goal of creating an inviting website that builds community.
